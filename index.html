Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started?
Sign up for an account, create your profile, and dive right in—whether you're looking to post gigs or find the perfect freelancer. In just a few easy steps, you can begin earning or getting work done efficiently. Join a community of talented professionals and clients ready to collaborate and achieve success together.
How are payment processed?
Payments are securely processed through our platform to ensure a smooth and reliable experience for both freelancers and clients. Once a gig is successfully completed, the payment is seamlessly transferred to the freelancer. Clients can conveniently pay using credit cards, cryptocurrency, or other supported payment methods, providing flexibility and peace of mind.
What happens if I have an issue?
If you encounter any issues, our dedicated support team is always ready to assist you. Whether you need help resolving disputes, have questions about the platform, or require guidance on navigating your projects, we are here to ensure everything runs smoothly. Our goal is to provide a hassle-free experience for both freelancers and clients.
